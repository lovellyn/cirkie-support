### Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**How do I create my own agent?**  
You can create your own agent by navigating to the "Gallery" tab and tapping the '+' button in the top right corner.

**How does the Cirkie Plus subscription work?**  
Cirkie Plus is an auto-renewing subscription that unlocks advanced features. You can manage your subscription at any time through your Apple ID account settings.
